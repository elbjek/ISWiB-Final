Take the

Wake up guys! Do not miss the perfect opportunity to experience 8 unforgettable days with the whole world in one place! Through art, science and practical work you will have a chance to think creatively about today’s burning topics. Let’s talk about the next step in the career of every one of you and look closely at global problems that surround us. It’s time to ask yourself: why wouldn’t YOU be the person that will push the limits and change the world? Belgrade has something to offer to everyone. We are proud to say that next summer we are the hosts of young leaders in our city. So, make room for ISWiB 2018 and spend an unforgettable summer in Belgrade! We can all agree that it is high time for new challenges, therefore let’s take the lead together!
